How it works

Selling

I will meet you at the lounge of the floor you specify. I will send you an email when I arrive. We will discuss prices. If we come to an agreement, then you will hand me the item and I will hand you the cash.

Buying

I will come to the lounge of the floor you specify. I will send you an email when I arrive. You will hand me the cash. Then I will give you the item.

Delivery

I will meet you in the lounge of the floor you specify. I will send you an email when I arrive. You will hand me 3 USD plus any money regarding what you want delivered. e.g. If you want something from The Den, you will need to hand me the cash to pay for it. I will deliver and send you an email once I am back to the same location we met the first time. If I can't retrieve what you wanted, you will recieve a full refund. Including the 3 dollar charge.
